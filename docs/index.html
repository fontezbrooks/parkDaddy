Privacy Policy

parkDaddy — by Fontez Brooks

Effective Date: April 2, 2026 Last Updated: April 2, 2026

Thank you for using parkDaddy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our mobile application. By using parkDaddy, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Overview

parkDaddy is a mobile application that automates guest parking registration at Ponce Springs Lofts through the ParkEaz parking management service. The app streamlines the process of registering guest vehicles so they are not ticketed or towed while parked at the property.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

When you use parkDaddy, we collect the following information:

  • First name and last name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Vehicle license plate number

2.2 Authentication Data

parkDaddy uses Clerk as its authentication provider. When you sign in with Google Single Sign-On (SSO), Clerk receives and manages your authentication credentials, including your Google account email and profile information. We do not store your Google password.

2.3 Push Notification Tokens

If you enable push notifications, we collect your device's push notification token through Expo Push Notifications in order to send you parking registration confirmations and relevant alerts.

2.4 Information We Do NOT Collect

parkDaddy does not collect location data, device identifiers for advertising, browsing history, or any other data beyond what is listed above. We do not use analytics or tracking tools, and we do not serve advertisements.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Parking Registration: To submit your guest vehicle information to ParkEaz on your behalf so the vehicle is properly registered at Ponce Springs Lofts.
  • Authentication: To verify your identity and manage your account through Clerk.
  • Notifications: To send you push notifications about the status of your parking registrations.
  • Account Management: To maintain your account and associated parking records within the app.

We do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise share your personal information with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

4. Third-Party Services

parkDaddy relies on the following third-party services, each of which has its own privacy policy governing how your data is handled:

  • ParkEaz — We submit your name, email, phone number, and license plate number to ParkEaz to register guest vehicles on your behalf. ParkEaz processes this data according to its own privacy policy and terms of service.
  • Clerk — Handles user authentication and account management, including Google SSO. Visit clerk.com/privacy for details.
  • Convex — Provides backend data storage and real-time database functionality for the app. Visit convex.dev/privacy for details.
  • Expo Push Notifications — Delivers push notifications to your device. Visit expo.dev/privacy for details.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Convex, a cloud-based backend service. We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as you maintain an active account with parkDaddy. When your account is deleted, your associated personal data will be removed from our systems.

Please note that data previously submitted to ParkEaz for parking registrations may be retained by ParkEaz in accordance with their own data retention policies.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: You may request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: You may update your information through the app or by contacting us.
  • Deletion: You may request deletion of your account and associated data by emailing us at fontez0622@gmail.com.
  • Notifications: You may disable push notifications at any time through your device settings.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided in the Contact section below.

If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete that information as promptly as possible.
